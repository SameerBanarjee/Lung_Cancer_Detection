Key Risk Indicators

  • Smoking is the #1 cause of lung cancer, responsible for ~85% of cases.
  • Chest pain & shortness of breath in smokers warrant immediate screening.
  • Persistent coughing lasting >3 weeks is a red-flag symptom.
  • Yellow fingers often indicate prolonged nicotine exposure.
  • Early detection raises the 5-year survival rate to over 56%.
